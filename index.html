  • MR Application Developer (Master’s Thesis)

    University of Agder

    November 2024 - May 2025, Grimstad


    Developed a Mixed Reality application with multimodal feedback (visual, auditory, and haptic) for training in abdominal palpation. The goal of the thesis was to explore how an immersive system can be designed and developed to support active learning among medical students and healthcare professionals, as well as to examine the extent to which visual, haptic, and auditory feedback influence the learning experience in medical training environments.
